Welcome to Acer Corner

Nestled in the beautiful Chiltern Hills in Wendover, Acer Corner is a unique garden showcasing one of the finest collections of Japanese maples (Acers) in Buckinghamshire.

Our garden spans 1.3 acres and has been carefully designed to highlight the stunning seasonal colors and graceful forms of over 120 different varieties of Acers. From the fiery crimsons and oranges of autumn to the delicate chartreuse and burgundy hues of spring, our garden offers a spectacular display year-round.

Garden Features

Beyond our signature Acer collection, visitors to Acer Corner will enjoy:

  • Tranquil water features including a koi pond and gentle streams
  • Meandering pathways leading through woodland areas
  • A dedicated Japanese garden section with authentic elements
  • Thoughtfully placed seating areas to admire the garden views
  • Seasonal displays of companion plants including hostas, ferns, and azaleas
