Welcome

Nestled in Wendover, Acer Corner is a garden designed and nurtured by a passionate garden designer, celebrated for its extensive collection of Japanese maples and serene Japanese-inspired style. The enclosed front garden evokes the tranquillity of Japan, while the back garden unfolds into three distinct areas, each offering its own character and seasonal interest.

Garden Features

  • Japanese-Style Front Garden: An enclosed space inspired by traditional Japanese gardens, offering calm and contemplative beauty.
  • Redesigned Patio Area: The patio has been recently transformed with Japanese design elements, perfect for relaxing and enjoying the view.
  • Densely Planted Borders: Discover a rich tapestry of acers and roses, providing vibrant colour and interest throughout the seasons.
  • Productive Greenhouse Corner: A tucked-away corner featuring a greenhouse and diverse, interesting planting.
